A Foundation in Medicine

Medicine, Trauma Care & Human Resilience

Dr. Allison Mulcahy is a double board-certified physician in Emergency Medicine and Integrative Medicine whose work spans emergency medicine, regenerative medicine, trauma physiology, somatic healing, and integrative health.

For more than 15 years, Allison worked in emergency departments ranging from major trauma centers to rural hospitals throughout the United States. Her work also included humanitarian and global health experiences in regions affected by disaster, conflict, and limited medical access.

These experiences profoundly shaped her understanding of suffering, resilience, the nervous system, and the deep complexity of being human.

Expanding the Model of Healing

Beyond Symptom Management

Over time, Allison became increasingly interested in the relationship between:

  • trauma
  • nervous system physiology
  • emotional health
  • embodiment
  • sexuality
  • longevity
  • regenerative medicine
  • consciousness

Her advanced training includes fellowships and education in:

  • Integrative Medicine
  • Functional Medicine
  • Regenerative Medicine
  • Precision Medicine
  • Integrative Psychiatry

In 2019, she founded Boost, an integrative and regenerative medicine practice focused on whole-system healing and nervous system restoration. This clinical background forms the scientific foundation for Awakening Aphrodite.

Bridging Disciplinary Pathways

Weaving Science & Somatics

Allison's journey has also been shaped by deep training and exploration in somatic experiencing, trauma healing, breathwork, emotional processing, embodiment arts, and consciousness studies.

She has spent years exploring how modern medical science and traditional somatic lineages can inform, ground, and enrich one another.

Awakening Aphrodite is the expression of this integration—a vision to build an embodied, community-centered future of healing.

The movement explores how medicine, nervous system healing, emotional truth, embodiment, community, land stewardship, feminine intelligence, and consciousness exploration might work together to support both personal and collective transformation.

At the heart of the work is a simple wish, one that has guided Allison since childhood: To help reduce unnecessary suffering and create spaces where people can reconnect with themselves, one another, nature, and life more fully.
